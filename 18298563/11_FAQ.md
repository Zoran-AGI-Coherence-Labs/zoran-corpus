FAQ

Is this political?
No. This work evaluates coherence only. It does not assess legitimacy, ideology, or intent.

Does global incoherence imply error?
No. It indicates a mismatch between local validity and systemic constraints.

Can local coherence and global incoherence both be true?
Yes. They apply to different levels: act vs trajectory.

What would falsify this?
A comparable case where corrective pressure converges toward stability despite the same structural patterns, under the same framework.
