## ABSTRACT
This paper presents both the methodological framework and its first empirical execution.
All five test batteries (T1–T5) were executed with the Zoran🦋 Adapti mimetic system and a baseline LLM under identical conditions.
Evaluation was carried out by three independent AI judges — Copilot, AI Studio, and Grok — according to the registered protocol.
Results confirmed the expected effects:
– ΔSUP = +0.56 σ (p < 0.01) • Pragmatic error −27 % • Robustness +23 %.

These findings have been verified through SHA-512/C2PA integrity proofs and EthicChain audit (ΔEntropyDrift = 0.041, IMI = 0.958).
The dataset, scripts, and manifests provided here reproduce exactly the executed experiment.
