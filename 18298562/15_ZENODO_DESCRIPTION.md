Zenodo Description

This record presents a global coherence evaluation of the dissolution of the French National Assembly announced on 9 June 2024.

Local coherence of the act is established independently (DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.18298563). This record evaluates global coherence as a trajectory over time and demonstrates global incoherence through systemic and temporal observation.

The work is non-partisan and non-normative. It assigns no intent, no responsibility, and proposes no alternatives.
