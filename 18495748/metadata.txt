Title:
Cohérence cognitive distribuée — Pourquoi certaines capacités humaines n’existent qu’en interaction

Creators:
Frédéric Tabary

Affiliation:
ZORAN🦋CφΩ

Description:
This white paper introduces a falsifiable, non-circular framework for distributed cognition based on phenomenological coherence.
Starting from a minimal, universal phenomenon (shared singing), it demonstrates across multiple domains that functional accessibility
depends on the maintenance of a coherent temporal trajectory beyond individual capacity.
